LucidXPay FAQ

What is a Web3 card?

A Web3 card is a digital payment card that allows you to spend your cryptocurrency seamlessly in the real world. It enables instant conversion of your crypto assets into local currency at the point of sale, making it easy to use your digital assets for everyday purchases.

How does the Web3 card work?

The Web3 card connects to your cryptocurrency wallet. When you make a purchase, the card automatically converts the required amount of cryptocurrency into the local currency, allowing you to pay just like you would with a traditional debit or credit card.

Which cryptocurrencies are supported?

The Web3 card supports a variety of popular cryptocurrencies, including Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), and many others. Please check our website for the most up-to-date list of supported cryptocurrencies.

Is the Web3 card secure?

Yes, the Web3 card is designed with security in mind. It uses advanced encryption and security protocols to protect your funds and personal information. Additionally, you have full control over your assets, and you can freeze or deactivate your card at any time through our app.

Are there any fees associated with using the Web3 card?

There may be transaction fees associated with converting your cryptocurrency to local currency, as well as monthly maintenance fees. Please refer to our fee schedule on the website for detailed information.

How can I load funds onto my Web3 card?

You can load funds onto your Web3 card by transferring cryptocurrency from your wallet to your card account. The process is simple and can be done through our mobile app or website.

Can I use the Web3 card internationally?

Yes, the Web3 card can be used internationally wherever major credit cards are accepted. However, please be aware of potential foreign transaction fees and currency conversion rates.

How do I track my transactions?

You can easily track your transactions through our mobile app or website. The app provides a detailed transaction history, allowing you to monitor your spending and manage your funds effectively.

What should I do if I lose my Web3 card?

If you lose your Web3 card, you should immediately freeze it through our mobile app to prevent unauthorized transactions. You can then request a replacement card through the app or customer support.

How do I contact customer support?

If you have any questions or need assistance, you can contact our customer support team via email, live chat, or through the support section of our mobile app. We’re here to help!
